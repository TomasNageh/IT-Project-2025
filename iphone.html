Design & display

The iPhone 16 and iPhone 16 Plus showcase a sleek design with a durable Ceramic Shield front cover, offering enhanced protection. Both models feature Super Retina XDR OLED displays measuring 6.1 inches and 6.7 inches, respectively, providing vibrant colors and deep blacks. The Dynamic Island integrates seamlessly, offering real-time alerts and activities. While the Pro models boast a 120Hz ProMotion display, the standard iPhone 16 models continue with a 60Hz refresh rate.

Dual lens camera

Equipped with a 48MP main camera, the iPhone 16 delivers high-resolution photos and supports a 2x optical-quality telephoto option. The 12MP Ultra Wide camera enables macro photography and captures more light for improved low-light performance. The new Camera Control feature allows users to quickly access camera settings and adjust parameters like zoom and exposure with ease.

Li-ion battery

The iPhone 16 houses a 3,561 mAh lithium-ion battery, while the iPhone 16 Plus comes with a 4,674 mAh battery. Both models support MagSafe wireless charging up to 25W and Qi2 wireless charging up to 15W. Fast charging capabilities allow up to 50% charge in approximately 30 minutes with a 20W adapter or higher.

A18 Bionic Chip

Powering the iPhone 16 lineup is the A18 chip, built on a second-generation 3nm process. This 6-core CPU (2 performance and 4 efficiency cores) offers up to 30% faster performance compared to the A16 Bionic, with improved energy efficiency. The integrated 16-core Neural Engine delivers 35 trillion operations per second, enhancing machine learning tasks and supporting Apple Intelligence features.
